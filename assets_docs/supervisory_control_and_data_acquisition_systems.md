# Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) Systems

# Summary
SCADA systems in maritime environments provide remote supervision and control over onboard propulsion, engine performance, and automation. Due to their central role in vessel operations and frequent integration with shore-based support systems, they represent high-impact targets for attackers seeking to disrupt physical control processes or override safety-critical functions.
