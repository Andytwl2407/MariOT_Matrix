# Vessel Management Systems (VMS)

# Summary
Vessel Management Systems are integrated shipboard platforms that coordinate navigation, cargo handling, and vessel automation through a combination of IT and OT subsystems.These systems often interface with remote maintenance platforms or shore-based control centers, making them high-value targets for attackers seeking to disrupt maritime operations or gain privileged access to onboard control systems.
