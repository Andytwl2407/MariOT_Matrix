# Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) & Supply Chain Systems

# Summary
ERP and supply chain systems coordinate maritime logistics, procurement, and financial operations across fleet, port, and supplier networks. These centralized platforms are tightly integrated with operational processes and external vendors, making them attractive targets for attackers seeking to disrupt fuel deliveries, cargo operations, or introduce malicious code through compromised supply chains.
