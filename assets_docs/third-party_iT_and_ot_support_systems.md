# Third-Party IT and OT Support Systems

# Summary
Third-Party IT and OT Support Systems External service providers are essential for the maintenance of maritime IT and OT systems, offering remote access for monitoring, diagnostics and technical support. However, these trusted connections can become points of vulnerability; If compromised, adversaries could gain access to or disrupt critical systems onboard OT, including navigation, power management, and propulsion control.
