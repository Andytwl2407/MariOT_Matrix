# Crew and Administrative Systems

# Summary
Crew and administrative systems handle sensitive HR data, payroll records, and access credentials for shipboard and shore-based personnel. Although not part of OT operations directly, these systems often store privileged accounts, VPN certificates, or directory credentials—making them attractive initial targets for attackers seeking to compromise maritime IT/OT environments through lateral movement or credential harvesting.
