# Integrated Communication Systems

# Summary
Integrated communication systems on vessels support both ship-to-shore and intra-vessel communications, including safety alerts, navigation coordination, and inter-crew signaling. These systems often rely on vendor-provided software or centralized control panels, which—if misconfigured or vulnerable—may be exploited to execute unauthorized code, disrupt emergency communications, or enable persistent remote access to maritime networks.
