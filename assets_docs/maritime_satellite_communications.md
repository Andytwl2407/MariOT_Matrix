# Maritime Satellite Communications

# Summary
Maritime satellite communication systems (e.g., VSAT, Inmarsat, Iridium) provide essential ship-to-shore connectivity for navigation, operations, and remote maintenance. These networks often serve as trusted channels for remote access and telemetry, but may be vulnerable to credential abuse, signal hijacking, or misconfigured trust relationships—making them critical pathways for potential OT intrusion.
