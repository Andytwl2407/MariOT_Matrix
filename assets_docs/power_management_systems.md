# Power Management Systems

# Summary
Power Management Systems control electrical distribution and automation aboard ships, ensuring stable power supply to key operational and safety systems. If compromised, attackers could inject startup execution mechanisms to manipulate or disable power automation, leading to potential blackouts, propulsion failures, or safety hazards.
