# Maritime Distress and Safety System

# Summary
Maritime distress and safety systems (e.g., GMDSS) provide essential communication capabilities for emergency signaling, vessel tracking, and distress coordination. These systems are mission-critical for crew safety, yet often rely on remotely accessible infrastructure or predefined communication protocols—making them potential targets for attackers seeking to suppress alerts, impersonate vessels, or exploit trusted communication channels.
