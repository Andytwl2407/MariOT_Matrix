# AIS-SART and MOB-AIS Devices

# Summary
AIS-SART (Search and Rescue Transmitter) and MOB-AIS (Man Overboard Beacon) are emergency AIS-based transmitters used for SAR operations. They broadcast distress signals to nearby vessels and shore stations.
