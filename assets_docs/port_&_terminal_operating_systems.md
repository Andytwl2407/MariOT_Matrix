# Port & Terminal Operating Systems (TOS)

# Summary
Port and Terminal Operating Systems (TOS) manage critical workflows across vessel arrival, berthing, cargo transfer, and customs clearance. These systems are often interconnected with shipping company platforms, customs APIs, and shore-based OT equipment—making them attractive targets for attackers aiming to disrupt port logistics or exploit weak authentication for lateral movement.
