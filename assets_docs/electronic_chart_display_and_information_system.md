# Electronic Chart Display and Information System (ECDIS)

# Summary
ECDIS is a digital navigation system that integrates chart data, GPS, AIS, and sensor inputs to support real-time route planning and maritime situational awareness. Its central role in bridge operations makes it a high-value target—if compromised, attackers could manipulate chart overlays or mislead navigators, leading to route deviations or grounding risks.
